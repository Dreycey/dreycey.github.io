EnrichSeq

  • Category: Bioinformatics
  • Technologies: Python, Bash, Nextflow
  • University/Client: CU Boulder
  • Project date(s): 2021-2022
  • Project URL: https://github.com/Dreycey/Phage-EnrichSeq

Description:

EnrichSeq is a bioinformatics pipeline aimed at classifying phages within a metagenomic dataset. An example of usage is finding and characterizing all bacteriophages in a sample from a human gut microbiome. This is unique as it is tailored specifically for finding bacteriophages, whereas most other pipelines focus on bacteria.
